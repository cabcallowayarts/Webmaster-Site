About Us

Our Mission

In 2020, five entrepreneurs saw the toll the COVID-19 pandemic was taking on themselves andothers as healthcare access became increasingly limited. They had siblings, cousins, and parents who found themselves at a loss as to how to proceed with vital pediatric care. So they set out to create their own solution.

Teletots is a company with an empathy-first approach. We believe in connecting doctors with patients safely in these unprecedented times. The doctor-patient relationship is one of the most sacred in healthcare, we will work tirelessly to make sure it is never compromised.

We are committed to keeping people healthy by providing them the spaces they need to connect their doctors and get the best possible care from their own homes. We strive to be a pioneer of new medical communication technologies as we move forward in thie every changing world.
