February 12, 2021

TeleTots Wins National Award for Best Pediatric Telemedicine

TeleTots wins the 2021 Calloway Award for the best new pediatric telemedicine company. In the era of the COVID-19 pandemic, TeleTots has demonstrated immense capability to create genuine connections between providers and patients to create safe, affordable, and asseible healthcare for thousands of children. Our company is so grateful for this award because we have consistently shown leadership and compassion in these unprecedented times.

As a result of this award, we are able to expand even further. We are planning to open up multiple new offices and partner with nonprofit organizations to expand our reach to norally underserved communities. We thank the Calloway Committee for their genorosity.

October 1, 2020

TeleTots Hires New CFO Holly Golder

TeleTots is excited to announce the inclusion of Holly Golder to our administrative team as Chief Financial Officer. Ms. Golder has been the financial chair of the Herbert Hills Foundation for Digital Accessibility since 2012 and in her position, invested hundreds of her hours in developing the nonprofit Herbert Hills Foundation into a worldwide phenomenon bringing digital literacy to all people regardless of economic success. She will begin her tenure at TeleTots October 15th, and we welcome her with open arms.

June 12, 2020

Pediatric Telemedicine Company TeleTots Opens

During this pandemic, we have seen the immense stress put onto the American healthcare system, especially when it comes to pediatric care. As a result, at TeleTots, our goal is to create a safe and effective way for kids to get the necessary healthcare.

At Teletots, we have partnered with four healthcare providers to provide efficient telemedicine to any customer. We have an app, a destop application, and are planning on so much more to serve any and all providers and recipients of pediatric healthcare. We have opened an office on Medical Highway in Wilmington Delaware. We will host an opening Zoom Q&A. Please email contact@teletots.org for the link and further details on that.
